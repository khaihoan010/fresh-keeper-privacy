If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from your Google account. We do not access your Google contacts, calendar, or other data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide core features: Track food expiry dates, manage inventory, send expiry notifications
  • Family sharing: Share food inventory data with family members you invite
  • Cloud sync: Synchronize your data across multiple devices (Premium feature)
  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns to improve the app
  • Advertising: Display ads to free-tier users via Google AdMob
  • Purchase processing: Process in-app purchases for Premium subscriptions
  • Notifications: Send expiry alerts and app updates

3. Family Sharing & Data Disclosure

Important: When you create or join a family group, your food inventory data, display name, and profile photo will be visible to all members of that family group.
  • Family groups are created by invitation using a unique 6-character code
  • Only members who join using the code can see shared data
  • Shared data includes: food products, expiry dates, quantities, storage locations, and who added each item
  • You can leave a family group at any time, which removes your access to shared data
  • The family admin can remove members and delete the family group
  • Your personal (non-family) data is never shared with family members

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Local storage: Your food inventory is stored locally on your device using SQLite database
  • Cloud storage: If you use cloud sync (Premium), data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore servers
  • Encryption: All data transmitted between the app and our servers uses TLS/SSL encryption
  • Profile photos: Uploaded photos are compressed (max 256x256 pixels) and stored in Firebase Storage
  • Data location: Cloud data is stored on Google Cloud infrastructure

6. Data Retention

  • Your data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted products are soft-deleted and permanently removed after 30 days
  • If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Analytics data is retained for up to 14 months as per Google Analytics defaults
  • Crash reports are retained for 90 days

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we have about you (available in the app's Settings)
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data (Settings → Data → Delete Account)
  • Export: Export your food inventory data (Settings → Data → Backup)
  • Opt-out of ads: Upgrade to Premium for an ad-free experience
  • Withdraw consent: You can stop using the app at any time
  • Correction: Update your profile information at any time in Settings

For EU/EEA Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to data portability, the right to restrict processing, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Our legal basis for processing is consent (for optional features) and legitimate interest (for core app functionality).

For California Users (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, and opt-out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell your personal information.

8. Children's Privacy

Fresh Keeper is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete such information.

9. In-App Purchases

Fresh Keeper offers Premium subscriptions (monthly, yearly, and lifetime plans) and Family plans. All payments are processed through Google Play Billing (Android) or Apple StoreKit (iOS). We do not collect or store any payment card information. Subscription management and cancellation are handled through your device's app store.

10. Advertising

Free-tier users may see advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier to show personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

Premium users do not see any advertisements.

11. Push Notifications

Fresh Keeper sends local notifications to alert you about expiring food items. These notifications are processed entirely on your device and do not send data to any server. You can manage notification preferences in the app's Settings or through your device's notification settings.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
